

# PCB-solder-mask-dat

- [[PCB-solder-mask-dat]] - [[PCB-dat]] - [[PCB-design-dat]] - [[PCB-vias-dat]]


KiCad treats the mask layers—F.Mask (Front Mask) and B.Mask (Back Mask)—as negative layers.

Anything you draw on F.Mask or B.Mask represents an opening in the solder mask.

If you draw a line or a polygon on F.Mask right over a copper track on F.Cu, KiCad will tell the factory to leave that area bare.
