## crank vs hinge 

| Item            | Crank                          | Connecting Rod                     |
|-----------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| Main Function   | Converts rotation to reciprocation (or reverse) | Transmits force between crank and other parts |
| Mounted on Shaft| Yes (must be fixed on a rotating shaft) | No (usually connected by pins/joints) |
| Shape           | Short arm with an offset       | Long straight rod                   |
| Role in Mechanism | Driving element (creates motion) | Linking element (passes motion/force) |
