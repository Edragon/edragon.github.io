Wiring 
- Y = TXD+ --- B = RXD+
- Z = TXD- --- A = RXD-
- B = RXD+ --- Y = TXD+
- A = RXD- --- Z = TXD-
or 
- A - Y 
- B - Z
- Y - A 
- Z - B

Definitions 
- Y = TXD+
- Z = TXD- 
- A = RXD-
- B = RXD+
