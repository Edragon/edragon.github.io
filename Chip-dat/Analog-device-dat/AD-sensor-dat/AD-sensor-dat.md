## AD7814

AD7814 - 10-Bit Digital Temperature Sensor in 6-Lead SOT-23

- –55°C to +125°C Operating Temperature Range
- 2°C Accuracy
- SPI- and DSP-Compatible Serial Interface
- Shutdown Mode
- Space-Saving SOT-23 Package

APPLICATIONS
- Hard Disk Drives
- Personal Computers
- Electronic Test Equipment
- Office Equipment
- Domestic Appliances
- Process Control
