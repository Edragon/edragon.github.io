
# CONN-qwiic-dat

- [[CONN-dat]] - [[CONN-qwiic-dat]]

The SparkFun Qwiic connection system utilizes a `1.0mm pitch`, 4-pin JST-SH connector. These connectors are specifically designed to be tiny, polarized (meaning they can only be plugged in one way), and reliable, making them ideal for quick, soldering-free I2C prototyping.
