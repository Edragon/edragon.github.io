WiFi 6, also known as 802.11ax, is the sixth generation of WiFi technology. It offers faster speeds, higher efficiency, and better performance in environments with many connected devices compared to previous WiFi standards. Key features include improved data rates, increased capacity, lower latency, and better power efficiency, making it ideal for dense environments like offices, stadiums, and smart homes.
