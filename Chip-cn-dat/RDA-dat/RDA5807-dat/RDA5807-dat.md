The RDA5807MS is a single-chip broadcast FM stereo radio tuner that integrates a synthesizer, RDS/RBDS decoder, and audio processing into a compact SOP-8 (Small Outline Package) form factor. It is commonly used in DIY radio builds, portable electronics, and microcontroller projects.

datasheet == [[RDA5807MS-datasheet.pdf]]



The RDA5807MS series is the newest generation single-chipbroadcast FM stereo radio tuner with fully integrated synthesizer IF selectivity, RDS/RBDS and MPX decoder. The tuner uses the CMOS process, support mulfinterface and require the least external component. All these make it very suitable for portabledevices.

The RDA5807MS series has a powerful low-IF digital audioprocessor, this make it have optimum sound quality with varying reception conditions.

The RDA5807MS series support frequency range is from 50MHz to 115MHz.
