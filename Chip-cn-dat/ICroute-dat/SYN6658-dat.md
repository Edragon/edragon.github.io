

# SYN6658-dat.md

The **SYN6658** is an LQFP64 SMD speech synthesis chip (10mm x 10mm, 0.5mm pitch).

## Overview
The SYN6658 is a high-cost-performance, high-end speech synthesis chip (TTS) released in 2012. It communicates via UART or SPI interfaces to receive text data and convert it into high-quality, natural-sounding speech.

Inheriting the strengths of the SYN6188 and SYN6288—such as its compact SMD package, simple hardware interface, low power consumption, and clear tone—the SYN6658 also features significantly smarter recognition of text, numbers, and strings. Its naturalness and intelligence represent a qualitative leap, making it ideal for high-end industrial applications.

## Key Features
- **Encoding Support:** Supports arbitrary Chinese text synthesis using GB2312, GBK, BIG5, and Unicode.
- **Intelligent Processing:** Advanced text analysis for numbers, phone numbers, date/time, and weights/measures.
- **Polyphone Support:** Automatically handles polyphonic words to ensure correct pronunciation.
- **Volume Control:** 10 levels of digital volume adjustment for high-volume output.
- **Built-in Sounds:** Includes 77 system prompt sounds and 14 chord melodies.
- **Multiple Voices:** Offers 6 voices: 2 male, 2 female, 1 effect voice, and 1 female child voice.
- **Control Tags:** Supports various text control tags to improve processing accuracy.
- **Command Set:** Includes synthesis, stop, pause, resume, and baud rate configuration.
- **Status Monitoring:** Multiple methods to query the chip's operational status.
- **Communication:** Supports both UART and SPI interfaces.
- **Power Management:** Features a PowerDown mode accessible via control commands.
- **Baud Rates:** Supports 4800, 9600, 57600, and 115200 bps.
- **Environmental Durability:** Suitable for use in harsh outdoor industrial environments.

## Application Scenarios
- **Automotive:** Vehicle information systems, scheduling, and navigation.
- **Public Transport:** Bus stop announcers and attendance machines.
- **Telecommunications:** Mobile phones and fixed-line systems.
- **Enterprise/Retail:** Queuing systems, cash registers, vending machines, and POS terminals.
- **Industrial/Utility:** Smart meters, weather warning systems, and smart transformers.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Smart toys, watches, and electric bicycles.
- **Education/Tourism:** E-books, storybooks, electronic dictionaries, and voice tour guides.
- **Messaging:** SMS playback and news broadcasting.
- **Navigation:** Electronic maps.
