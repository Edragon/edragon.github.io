

# SYN6288-dat



- [[TTS-dat]] - [[audio-dat]]

- [[SYN6288-dat]] - [[SYN6658-dat]]


The **SYN6288** is a compact, high-quality Chinese text-to-speech (TTS) chip designed for embedded applications. It supports multiple text encodings and provides clear, natural, and accurate speech synthesis with intelligent text processing and flexible control options.

## Key Features
- **Encoding Support:** GB2312, GBK, BIG5, and Unicode.
- **High-Quality TTS:** Clear, natural, and accurate Chinese speech synthesis; supports synthesis of arbitrary Chinese text and Latin letters.
- **Intelligent Text Analysis:** Algorithms detect and correctly handle numbers, phone numbers, dates/times, and common units of measure.
- **Polyphone & Name Handling:** Strong multi-pronunciation (polyphone) processing and improved handling for Chinese surnames.
- **Text Control Tags:** Supports various control tags to improve processing accuracy.
- **Max Text Length:** Up to 200 bytes per synthesis request.
- **Control Commands:** Synthesis, stop, pause, resume, change baud rate, and other control commands.
- **Power Saving:** Sleep mode reduces power consumption; supports multiple ways to query chip status.
- **Serial Interface & Baud Rates:** Serial data interface supporting 9600, 19200, and 38400 bps.
- **Volume Control:** 16-level volume adjustment; separate control for foreground (speech) and background (music) volumes.
- **Speech Speed:** Adjustable via control tags; supports 6 speed levels.
- **Built-in Audio Assets:** On-chip prompts, chord melodies, and industry-specific voice prompts.
- **Integrated Sounds:** 19 system prompt sounds, 23 chord prompt sounds, and 15 background music tracks.
- **Package & Size:** Final product available in SSOP SMD package; among the smallest in the industry.
- **Environmental Durability:** Specifications meet requirements for harsh outdoor environments.

## Typical Applications
- Public announcement systems and bus stop announcers
- Attendance and access control devices
- Consumer electronics and toys
- Vending machines, POS systems, and kiosks
- Smart instruments, meters, and industrial monitoring

## Notes
- Use the provided control commands and tags to tailor pronunciation, speed, and volume for specific use cases.
- Verify baud rate and power mode settings in your hardware design to ensure reliable communication and low-power behavior.





【功能介绍】
- *清晰、自然、准确的中文语音合成效果；可合成任意的中文文本，支持英文字母的合成；
- *具有智能的文本分析处理算法，可正确识别数值、号码、时间日期及常用的度量衡符号；
- *具备很强的多音字处理和中文姓氏处理能力；
- *支持多种文本控制标记，提升文本处理的正确率；
- *每次合成的文本量最多可达200字节；
- *支持多种控制命令，包括：合成、停止、暂停合成、继续合成改变波特率等；
- *支持休眠功能，在休眠状态下可降低功耗；支持多种方式查询芯片工作状态；
- *支持串行数据通讯接口，支持三种通讯波特率：9600BPS，19200BPS、38400BPS;
- *支持16级音量调整；播放文本的前景音量和播放背景音乐的背景音量可分开控制；
- *可通过发送控制标记调节词语语速，支持6级词语语速调整；
- *芯片内固化有多首和弦音乐、提示音效和针对某些行业领域的常见语音提示音；
- *内部集成19首声音提示音，23首和弦提示音，15首背景音乐；
- *最终产品提供SSOP贴片封装形式；体积业内最小；
- *芯片各项指标均满足室外严酷环境下的应用；
