## Physical Jack Type

**3.5 mm TRS (Stereo) jack**

- Tip = Left audio
- Ring = Right audio
- Sleeve = Ground

Electrical Pinout (Headphone Jack)

    ┌──── Tip ──── Left Audio (AF-L)
    │
    │ ┌── Ring ─── Right Audio (AF-R)
    │ │
    │ │ ┌ Sleeve ─ Ground (GND)
    ▼ ▼ ▼
    [ T | R | S ]


### Signal Characteristics

| Item | Value |
|----|----|
| Signal type | Analog audio |
| Level | ~0.5–1 Vrms (max) |
| Impedance | ~16–32 Ω load |
| AC-coupled | Yes |
| DC safe | No (AC only) |
