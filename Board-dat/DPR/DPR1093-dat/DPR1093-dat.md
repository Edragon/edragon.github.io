
# DPR1093 dat

## version 2

![](10-17-16-22-08-2023.png)

- SW1: switch power supply 5V or 3.3V
- Mode Select: UART mode or all other modes (IIC/SPI EPP/MEM ENBALE)

- RDY LED: ready 
- TNOW LED: TNOW

- Supported Interface: SPI I2C UART, parallel 


## Mode 

### UART

- RDY_LED ON


### Other 

- TNOW_LED ON
