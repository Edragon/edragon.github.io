Working voltage:
- R 1.9 – 2.1 V
- G 3.0 -3.2V
- B 3.0 – 3.2V

Working current: 52-55 mA

Product lightness:
- R 400-500
- G 1000-1200
- B 300-400 (MCD)

Product wavelength:
- R 620-625
- G 520-525
- B 465 – 470

Working angle:130-140 degree

Notice: solder temperature should not higher than 260 degree

Pin 1, 2, 3 on > side is GND, and pin 4, 5, 6 is RGB VCC in.
