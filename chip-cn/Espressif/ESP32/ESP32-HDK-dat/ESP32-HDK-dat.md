
# ESP32 HDK dat

## pins 

### RMT 

- RMT_SIG_IN0~7
- Any GPIO Pins
- Eight channels for an IR transmitter and
- RMT_SIG_OUT0~7 receiver of various waveforms

- [[infrared-dat]]



### Serial 

- U0 = RXD0 / TXD0
- U1 = SD2 / SD3 (can not use)
- U2 = U2RXD = IO16, U2TXD = IO17
