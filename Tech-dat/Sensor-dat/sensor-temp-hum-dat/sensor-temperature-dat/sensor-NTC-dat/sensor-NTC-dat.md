
# NTC-sensor-dat

- NTC10D-11

- Type: Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) Thermistor
- Resistance: 10 kΩ at 25°C
- B Value: ~3435 K (typical)
- Max Voltage: 250V AC
- Max Current: 2.5A
- Operating Temperature: −40°C to +125°C
- Tolerance: ±5% (at 25°C)
- Application: Inrush current limiter, temperature sensing
