## What Does 24Wh Mean in Practice?

To give you a physical sense of how much energy 24瓦时 (Wh) represents, a fully charged 12V 2Ah battery can power:

*   A **12W** LED light bulb for **2 hours** (12W * 2h = 24Wh).
*   A **24W** DC motor running at full load for **1 hour** (24W * 1h = 24Wh).
*   A small **6W** electronic circuit or microcontroller setup for **4 hours** (6W * 4h = 24Wh).
