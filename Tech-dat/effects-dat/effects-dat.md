## thermoelectric system

- **Seebeck Effect** — Temperature difference generates voltage.  - [[Seebeck-dat]]
  *(Heat → Electricity)*

- **Peltier Effect** — Electric current causes heating or cooling at junctions.  - [[Peltier-dat]]
  *(Electricity → Heat flow)*

- **Thomson Effect** — In a single conductor, current + temperature gradient causes heat absorption or release.  - [[Thomson-dat]]
  *(Electricity + Temp gradient → Heat)*

- **Ettingshausen Effect** — Temperature gradient + magnetic field → voltage (perpendicular).  
  *(Heat + Magnetism → Electricity)*

- **Nernst Effect** — Magnetic field + voltage → temperature difference.  
  *(Electricity + Magnetism → Heat)*
