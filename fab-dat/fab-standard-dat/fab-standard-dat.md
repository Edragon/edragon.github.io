## SOP 

**SOP (Standard Operating Procedure)** is a **documented set of clear, step-by-step instructions** that explains **how to perform a task correctly and consistently**.

### Purpose of SOP
- Ensure **consistent quality**
- Reduce **errors and risks**
- Improve **efficiency**
- Support **training**
- Meet **safety and compliance** requirements

### In Simple Words
> SOP = **The standard way everyone should do the same job**

Used widely in **manufacturing, electronics, healthcare, IT, and business operations**.
