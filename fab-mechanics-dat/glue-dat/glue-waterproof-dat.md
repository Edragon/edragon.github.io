Recommended Adhesives for Waterproof Sealing

1. **Epoxy Resin**
   - Two-part epoxy (clear or marine-grade)  
   - Creates a **strong, waterproof bond**  
   - Good for ABS, PETG, PLA  

2. **Plastic Solvent Welding**
   - For ABS: **acetone** melts edges and fuses pieces  
   - Creates a **solid, almost seamless bond**  

3. **Silicone Sealant**
   - Food-grade or marine silicone  
   - Not strong structurally, but excellent for **sealing gaps and making watertight**
