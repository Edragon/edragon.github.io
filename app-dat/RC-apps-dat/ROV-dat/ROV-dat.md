
# ROV-dat

**ROV** stands for **Remotely Operated Vehicle** — an underwater robot controlled from the surface using a tethered cable.

## What ROVs Are Used For
- Underwater filming & photography  
- Marine research  
- Inspecting ship hulls, pipelines, underwater structures  
- Search and rescue  
- Deep-sea exploration  

## Key Features
- **Tether cable** – delivers power and sends video/data  
- **Cameras & lights** – real-time viewing  
- **Thrusters** – movement in all directions  
- **Protective frame** – shields electronics  
- **Optional modules** – robotic arm, sonar, depth sensors, etc.
