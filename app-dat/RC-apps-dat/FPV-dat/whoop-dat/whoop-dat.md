## whoop-micro 65x 

- [[mobula6-dat]] - [[battery-1S-dat]]

The [[Happymodel-dat]] Mobula6 belongs to the 65mm Tiny Whoop category (often referred to as the 65x class or Micro FPV Whoop).  

Here is exactly how its category breaks down in the FPV drone world:

**Size Class (65mm)**: The "6" in Mobula6 stands for its 65mm wheelbase (the diagonal distance from the center of one motor to the center of the opposite motor). This is the absolute classic standard size for indoor micro drones.  

**Propeller Size**: It uses 31mm propellers enclosed in protective ducts (or ducts built into the frame), which defines it as a true "Whoop."  

**Power/Battery Category**: It is a 1S Brushless Whoop, meaning it runs on a single-cell (1S) LiPo/LiHV battery (typically 300mAh to 350mAh) paired with tiny brushless motors rather than older brushed variants.  

**Primary Use Case**: It is categorized as an Indoor Micro FPV / Whoop Racing drone, built specifically to maneuver tightly inside houses, through tight gates, or around obstacles safely.
