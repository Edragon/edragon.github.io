

# voltage-low-alarm-dat

Low-Voltage Alarm (LVA)

For DIY projects where you don't want the battery to suddenly cut off (like a drone or a robot), a Low-Voltage Alarm is common.

It plugs into the balance lead and displays the voltage of each cell.

It emits a loud beep when any cell hits a dangerous threshold (e.g., 3.3V), signaling you to manually turn off the device.
