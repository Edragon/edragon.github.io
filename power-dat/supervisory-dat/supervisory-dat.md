The STM809/810/811/812 MICROPROCESSOR RESET Circuits are low-power supervisory devices used to monitor power supplies. 

They perform a single function: asserting a reset signal whenever the VCC supply voltage drops below a preset value and keeping it asserted until VCC has risen above the preset...
